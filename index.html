This game uses a scientific learning algorithm that you can find here.

How to play:

  • Think about the name of the m33 you see.
  • Press enter or space to reveal his/her real name.
  • Using the arrow up and down keys, select how well you remembered his/her name this time i.e. do not select the quality of your long-term memory but the quality of your short term one.
  • Press enter or space to validate your choice and go to the next m33.
  • Each day, a new set of m33 is presented to you based on your last session.
  • The game ends when you have answered at least 4 for each m33 that is presented to you.
